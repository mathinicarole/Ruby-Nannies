Frequently Asked Questions

What types of services do your nannies provide?

Our nannies offer a variety of services including part-time care, live-in care, special needs care, travel assistance, newborn specialist services, multilingual support, and tutoring.

How do you screen your nannies?

All our nannies undergo a rigorous screening process that includes background checks, reference verification, and thorough interviews to ensure they meet our high standards of care.

Can I request a nanny with specific skills or qualifications?

Yes, we can match you with nannies who have the specific skills or qualifications you need, such as language fluency, special needs training, or tutoring experience

What is the process for hiring a nanny?

The process involves an initial consultation to understand your needs, followed by a selection of suitable candidates for you to interview. Once you choose a nanny, we assist with contract finalization and onboarding.

How are nannies matched to families?

We consider factors such as your childcare requirements, preferred schedule, and any special requests to match you with a nanny whose skills and availability align with your needs.

What if I need a nanny for short-term or last-minute care?

We offer flexible arrangements, including short-term and emergency nanny services, to accommodate your immediate childcare needs.

Can I interview nannies before making a decision?

Absolutely. We encourage families to interview potential nannies to ensure a good fit and to discuss expectations and preferences directly.
